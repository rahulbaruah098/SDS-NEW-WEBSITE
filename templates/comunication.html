    Communication for Development

    Communication for Development (C4D) blends strategic communication, social & behavior change, community mobilization, and advocacy to enable people to participate in decisions that affect their lives, adopt healthy and productive practices, and hold systems accountable. SDS designs end-to-end C4D programs—from insight to impact—rooted in local culture, languages, and channels people trust.

    What C4D Does

    Uses evidence-based messaging and two-way dialogue to address barriers—knowledge, social norms, access, and agency. It aligns message, messenger, medium, and moment to catalyze behavior and systems change in health, agriculture, education, nutrition, WASH, gender, and livelihoods.

    Core Pillars

    SBCC: Social & Behavior Change Communication for daily practices.
    Social Mobilization: Community leaders, SHGs, FPOs, and youth groups co-lead action.
    Advocacy: Data-driven narratives influence policies and budgets.
    Participatory Communication: Co-creation workshops and feedback loops ensure ownership.

    Channels & Formats

    Interpersonal counseling, home visits, farmer field schools, community radio, folk media, street plays, school programs, IVR & voice messages, WhatsApp chatbots, SMS drips, social media micro-content, IEC materials (posters, flipbooks), and short films in local languages.

    Strategy & Design

    Audience segmentation (primary/secondary/influencers), barrier analysis (knowledge, norms, access), message framing (gain-framed, norm-nudges), creative testing, media mix modeling, and content calendars. SDS aligns timing with agricultural and festival calendars to maximize reach and resonance.

    Implementation & Capacity Building

    Training frontline workers and community facilitators, creating toolkits, and establishing peer-educator networks. SDS equips CLFs, SHGs, and FPOs to run meetings, demonstrations, and community scorecards for sustained local ownership.

    Measurement, Learning & Adaptation

    KAP surveys, pre/post tests, exposure tracking, call-center analytics, WhatsApp engagement, IVR completion, and outcome indicators (adoption, referrals, service uptake). Rapid learning cycles refine messages and media mixes.

    1. Discover (formative research & insights)
    2. Co-create (with CLFs/SHGs/FPOs)
    3. Produce (multilingual content & toolkits)
    4. Mobilize (media mix & community events)
    5. Measure (KAP, uptake & feedback loops)
