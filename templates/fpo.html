    FPO Management & Development

    Empowering Rural Communities Through Collective Enterprise

    We unite smallholder farmers in Eastern and North-Eastern India under Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) to create sustainable livelihoods and drive rural development.

    Where We Come In

    SDS's core focus revolves around mitigating vulnerability through the promotion of collectivization. We manage and develop community enterprises to foster sustainable businesses and entrepreneurial ventures at the grassroots level.

    Our approach focuses on empowering rural communities, particularly smallholder farmers, through the formation and promotion of community-owned enterprises such as Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) and Producer Groups (PGs). These collectives help in advancing rural development and creating sustainable solutions to rural livelihood challenges.

    By uniting small and marginal farmers under a common umbrella, we enhance their bargaining power, improve access to markets, and increase their income potential while reducing individual risk.

    Our Comprehensive Support

    Knowledge, Skill & Technology

    We provide training programs and access to modern agricultural technologies to enhance productivity and efficiency.

    Income Enhancement

    Our programs focus on increasing farmers' income through better practices, value addition, and market linkages.

    Marketing Support

    We help FPOs access better markets, negotiate fair prices, and develop branding for their products.

    Farm Equipment

    We facilitate access to modern farming equipment through shared resource centers and equipment banks.

    Financial Services

    We connect farmers with financial institutions for credit, savings, and other financial services.

    Insurance Solutions

    We provide access to crop and health insurance products to mitigate risks and protect livelihoods.
