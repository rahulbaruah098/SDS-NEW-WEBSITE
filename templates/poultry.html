    Stronger Poultry Value Chains

    From brooding to market, SDS supports producers with practical know-how and services—reducing risk, improving productivity, and connecting farmers to buyers for assured off-take.

    Health & Biosecurity

    Sanitary entry, litter management, vaccination calendar, and water sanitation lower disease pressure.

    Climate-Smart Sheds

    Ventilation, brooding heat management, and drinker hygiene reduce stress and improve growth rates.

    Feed Efficiency

    Balanced rations and clean lines improve FCR and weights—cutting costs per kg liveweight.

    Assured Markets

    Cluster aggregation and buy-back tie-ups enable predictable, transparent pricing for farmers.
