    Institution Building AND Capacity Building

    Formation and promotion of community-owned organizations of smallholder farmers/ marginalized communities/ Self Help Group (SHG) women is a major focus area of SDS to build sustainable community organizations at the grassroots. Collaborating closely with both government and non-government entities, SDS supports the formation, development and capacity building of community groups such as Cluster Level Federations (CLFs) and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs).

    Institution Building & Capacity Building

    SDS forms and strengthens community-owned organizations—FPOs, PGs, SHGs, and CLFs—so smallholders and women collectives gain scale, voice, and better access to markets, finance, and resources.

    Formation of Community-Based Organizations

    Collaborating with government and non-government partners, SDS catalyzes the formation, development, and capacity building of Cluster Level Federations (CLFs), Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs), Producer Groups (PGs), and Self Help Groups (SHGs). These collectives enable economies of scale, stronger bargaining power, and inclusive access to value chains.

    Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)

    Collectivizing smallholders to aggregate, add value, and negotiate better prices while improving market access and financial linkages.

    Self Help Groups (SHGs)

    Women-led savings and enterprise platforms that build financial resilience, peer learning, and dignified livelihood opportunities.

    Cluster Level Federations (CLFs)

    Federated structures that strengthen SHGs and community institutions with governance support, services, and market integration.

    Producer Groups (PGs)

    Commodity-focused groups driving productivity, quality, and value addition to meet buyer requirements and reduce transaction costs.

    Capacity Building for Individuals & Organizations

    Training Programs

    SDS conducts ToTs and enterprise development trainings on financial management, governance, market linkages, and value-chain management—enabling leaders to cascade knowledge across their communities.

    Skill Development for Farmers

    Hands-on modules support climate-resilient agriculture, pest and risk management, and value-added processing to boost productivity and resilience.

    Leadership & Governance

    Focused coaching on transparent record-keeping, statutory compliance, and accountable decision-making helps organizations operate as sustainable enterprises.
