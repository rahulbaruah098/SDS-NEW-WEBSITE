    Our Mission

    SDS is dedicated to providing enabling services to organizations and individuals, to create potentials that positively affect the lives of the disadvantaged sections of society. We achieve this through a team of highly qualified personnel with extensive practical experience working at the grassroots level.

    Operating in 11 states across India, including all Northeast India states, Bihar, Odisha, and Karnataka, SDS serves the needs of Central and State governments, CSR agencies, not-for-profits, FPOs, individual entrepreneurs, and other organizations in the development sector.

    11 States Grassroots Experts CSR & Govt Partners Impact Focused

    Our Vision

    SDS aims to be a top-tier development support organization, promoting rural livelihoods in Eastern and North-Eastern India by facilitating livelihood ecosystems. Being among the best consulting firms in the Northeast, we strive to provide our top consultancy services in every nook and corner of the region.

    Our Values

    SDS is committed to fostering a culture that prioritizes excellence, expertise, and community engagement. We stand for values of:
    Equity- Justness, fairness, and equality of rights and opportunities.
    Integrity- Alignment in thought, word and action.
    Excellence- Excellence at every step for our clients and customers
    Trust- Firm belief in the reliability, truth, or ability of someone or something.
