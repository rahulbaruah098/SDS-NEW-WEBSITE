    IT CONSULTING SERVICES — SDS

    Transforming Organizations Through Technology, Innovation & Human-Centered Design

    Sayanant Development Services (SDS) delivers comprehensive IT consulting and digital transformation services designed to accelerate institutional efficiency, transparency, and innovation. We partner with NGOs, enterprises, FPOs, government missions, and mission-driven organizations to architect robust, scalable, and secure technology solutions tailored to complex field realities. Our capabilities include full-stack engineering, cloud-native architectures, enterprise MIS/ERP platforms, AI-powered automation, cyber-security hardening, systems integration, mobile and web experience design, and advanced analytics. Driven by a human-centered approach and engineering excellence, SDS empowers organizations to modernize processes, enhance governance, strengthen data-driven decision-making, and achieve sustainable digital maturity.

    WHY CHOOSE SDS
    What We Offer
    Web Development Services
    Custom websites (corporate, NGO, e-commerce, portals) with modern UI/UX and full-stack web applications with scalable backend architecture. SEO-optimized, mobile-responsive, high-performance sites with secure payment gateway integration, admin dashboards, user management and workflow automation tools, plus ongoing maintenance and optimisation.
    Mobile App Development
    Android & iOS apps (hybrid and native) for customers, field staff and program teams — including data collection and beneficiary-facing apps. Real-time sync with central MIS, offline-first features for low-network locations and smart push notifications for workflows and alerts.
    MIS, ERP & Enterprise Systems
    MIS for agriculture, livelihood, education, health and governance built on domain-driven design and robust data models. ERP modules — HRMS, payroll, inventory, finance and CRM — with data automation, approval workflows and multi-role dashboards for leadership, donors and field teams.
    AI & Automation
    Predictive analytics, advisory tools and chatbots using NLP and computer vision integrated with your MIS and data. Automation of routine workflows, reminders and risk flags so teams can focus on decision-making instead of manual follow-up.
    AWS & Cloud Infrastructure
    AWS setup, configuration and deployment with SSL, firewalls, automated backups, monitoring and cost optimisation. Nginx, Gunicorn, Docker pipelines and CI/CD for stable, repeatable deployments and minimal downtime for mission-critical systems.
    SEO, Branding, Security & AMC
    SEO strategy, branding assets and UI/UX design systems aligned with your organization’s identity. Secure authentication, RBAC, server hardening and data security protocols. SMS/OTP, payment gateways, API integrations and email automation, with documentation, training, helpdesk support and AMC.
