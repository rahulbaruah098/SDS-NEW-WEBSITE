    Research & Mapping · Manipur

    Scoping the FPO–Cooperative Ecosystem in Manipur

    Infrastructure gaps · hilly terrain logistics Weak governance · compliance support Limited credit access · collateral constraints Natural disasters · resilience planning CBBO & POPI handholding Market linkage · value chain development Smallholders · inclusive growth Infrastructure gaps · hilly terrain logistics

    This study explores the growing role of FPOs and cooperatives in Manipur, where agriculture sustains nearly 70% of households amid persistent constraints—poor infrastructure, market disconnects, and frequent natural disasters. Through field surveys, interviews with 20 FPOs, and stakeholder consultations, we map operations, assess performance, and identify systemic gaps in governance, finance, and institutional support to inform policy and practice for sustainable, inclusive rural development.

    20 FPOsInterviewed across select clusters
    ~70% HouseholdsDepend on agriculture
    Ecosystem PartnersFPCs · CBBOs · POPIs · Cooperatives

    Scope of Work & Strategic Focus

    From scoping to strategy, the assignment covers institutional diagnostics, market linkage assessments, governance and compliance review, finance–insurance access, capacity-building pathways via CBBOs/POPI, and a practical roadmap to strengthen producer organizations across Manipur’s varied agro-ecologies.

    Landscape Mapping

    Geo-tagged inventory of FPOs/FPCs/Coops with status of registration, membership, activities, and service areas.

    Governance & Compliance

    Board processes, audits, by-laws, statutory filings, and institutional health checks with corrective actions.

    Market & Value Chains

    Input aggregation, output marketing, primary processing, branding, and linkage with regional/national buyers.

    Finance & Insurance Access

    Credit readiness, SHG/JLG linkages, PMFBY uptake, and customized financial products for working capital needs.

    Capacity Building (CBBO/POPI)

    Targeted training modules for boards, CEOs, and member farmers—technical, managerial, and digital skilling.

    Policy & Institution Gaps

    Recommendations to align schemes, simplify processes, and improve convergence across departments and partners.
